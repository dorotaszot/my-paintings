Hi! I'm Dorota and I love watercolours. I've been painting with oils and acrylics ever since but I discovered my passion for watercolours a few years ago. I love watercolours and their unpredicatability as well as the discipline they require.

I love the feeling of a blank page and all the possibilities it gives me. The creation from scratch, letting my creative self thrive is what gives me most joy. I love experimenting with colours, brushes, paper types and paying attention to details when giving my pictures the final touch.

Apart from paintining I love yoga, travelling and recently started to learn to code. I find the feeling of an empty screen at the begining of the project very similary to a blank white page when I begin to paint. Possibilites are endless and the skills to master infinite. Learning and expanding are what I live for.
